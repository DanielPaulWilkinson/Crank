What Are pedals?

pedals are how the bike moves, using the power of your legs this provides the power to the bikes wheel depending on the bike this can be the back, front or both however most commonly the back.

History

The first pedals where called Lallement and created in 1860's these where the first patiented pedals with a counter balence to keep them in the right position. A more recent verson contains a counter weight in half the size, weight and double the flexability made with light but durable metals.
