What Are Frames?

A bike frame is the foundation to the bike. to which we can add to and customise how we want our bike to look. their are many bike frame types such as: dimond, BMX, prone etc... a bike frame that a user picks normally represents a use or an advantage in that use. for example a BMX is used commonly for skating. a mountain bike frame for rocky terain.

History

The first type of bike frame recorded in history was created in 1790 and called a celerifere. the design was later added to with a steering bar attached to the frount wheel, the model was named a draisienne. its a fun fact to think that it wasnt long ago bikes where made from basic materials such as wood and cast iorn however today made from exotic materials such as aluminum and titanium.
