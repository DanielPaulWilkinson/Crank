Recommended Trails

Quercus Trail

This fantastic blue grade route is 7.5km long with a shorter 3.5 km option. It is a gem waiting to be discovered, expect flowing single track with gentle berms , rolling jumps, wide gradual climbs with technical features for the adventurous riders.

Goosey Foot Tarn Trail

The shortest of our waymarked cycle trails, but there are still small hills involved, so be warned! The trail takes you past Goosey Foot and Juniper Tarns, two of our twenty or so man-made tarns. Park at Moor Top car park and follow the blue waymarkers. This short family cycle ride can be extended by linking with the Hawkshead Moor route.

Lakeside Way Cycle Trail

The Lakeside Way multi-user path offers visitors to Kielder Water & Forest Park a truly unique experience giving access to the most scenic parts of the lake shore and the opportunity to get up close to some of Kielderâ€™s abundant wildlife. The 26-mile path encircles Kielder Water and is suitable for walkers, cyclists, horse riders, mobility scooters and wheelchair users.

Instructions

1. Creating a test

To Create a test you must go to the "Create Tests" link, click it and then select a date so you know which date that your test was on. you then must fill in the rest of the form enusuring you dont miss one box. once you have you will notice a notification appear at the bottom, this means your test has been saved and you now have created your checkup.

2. Previewing a test

To preview a test click the "Preview Tests" link, from here all your tests will be listed however if you dont have any only the title will be placed there.

3. Navagating

To navagate we have decided to include both a Left menu which opens on the click of the burger on the right of the menu text, we have inclded important links on the bottom tool bar as well just so you find it easy to get around. their is also links on the home screen inside of the text.

4. Finding our recommended bike parts

To find our bike parts onpen the side menu, click bike parts and locate a type of bike part you would like to see, we have 5 buttons spanning off to different types. Click one of these buttons to be redirected to a page with our favourate and top 10 (in our opinion) bike parts, to futher learn fo their information click on a name and you will see therelating data
