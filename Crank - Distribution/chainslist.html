What Are Chains?

Your bicycle chain is a rolling chain that transfers power to either the back or frount wheele from the pedals, thus propelling it. Mostly, the bike chain is made from either alloy steel or carbon however some are nickel plated to prevent rust or just for the look.

History

Their was many types of chain designs before the 1908s, some included the 'block chain', 'skip-link chain' & the 'simpson lever chain.' From the 1980's the main type of chain used was "sedis" bushingless design. which is split into two types, brushless and brush. a brushing chain was cheaper to make, very efficent however wore very quickly which is different to the brushless. A fun fact is tha the pedals where attached to the center of the front wheel. today for saftey we attach the pedals to the center of the bike.
